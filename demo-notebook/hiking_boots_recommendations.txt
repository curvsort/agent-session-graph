# Hiking Boots Recommendations

I'd be happy to help you find the right hiking boots! To give you the best recommendations, let me share some top options across different categories:

## Top Overall Picks

**1. Salomon X Ultra 4 GTX**
- **Best for:** Day hiking and light backpacking
- **Features:** Waterproof Gore-Tex, excellent ankle support, aggressive traction
- **Price range:** $150-180
- **Weight:** Lightweight and nimble

**2. Merrell Moab 3 Mid Waterproof**
- **Best for:** Budget-conscious beginners
- **Features:** Comfortable out-of-box, reliable waterproofing, durable
- **Price range:** $130-160
- **Great value option**

**3. La Sportiva Spire GTX**
- **Best for:** Technical terrain and experienced hikers
- **Features:** Superior stability, climbing-zone toe, Italian craftsmanship
- **Price range:** $200-230

**4. Lowa Renegade GTX Mid**
- **Best for:** Multi-day backpacking with heavy loads
- **Features:** Exceptional support, very durable, European comfort fit
- **Price range:** $230-280

## Key Factors to Consider

- **Terrain type:** Day trails vs. rocky/technical terrain
- **Load weight:** Light day pack vs. multi-day backpacking gear
- **Climate:** Waterproof vs. breathable mesh
- **Fit:** Wide, narrow, or standard widths available

- What type of hiking are you planning?
